### :information_source: About
The **Software Discovery Engine** is a tool I've designed and built to help businesses find software that solves everyday problems or empowers company initiatives.

A relatively lightweight framework encapsulates a wealth of knowledge I've accumulated over the last nine-plus years working in the SaaS world. Like UX/UI design, software development, and aligning business needs with technical solutions.

The recommendations made here are mostly comprised of Utah SaaS companies.
